ISCVAM – Interactive Single-cell Visual Analytics for Multiomics

ISCVAM is an interactive visual analytics platform designed for single-cell multiome data. It enables researchers to explore and interpret complex single-cell datasets through:
- Multi-modal visualization – Integrate sc-RNA and sc-ATAC data to study both transcriptomic and epigenetic profiles of cells.
- Flexible clustering – Apply multiple single-cell clustering resolutions to identify rare or subtle cell populations.
- Cross-dataset comparison – Simultaneously examine up to three datasets to validate findings and ensure reproducibility.
